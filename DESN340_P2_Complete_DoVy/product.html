Shades of Grey

No. 2 Pencil makes sultry shades of grey. Gently rub the flat edge of the pencil tip back and forth to create contrast between light and dark. Smudge with your finger or side of your hand to get dirty. Chiaroscuro
never felt so good.

Erase

No. 2 Pencil comes with a standard rubber eraser that lets you remove misspellings and wrong answers.
Note: erasers are non-replaceable. Please order more than one No. 2 Pencil if you a make a lot of mistakes.

School Bus Yellow

A writing tool for the masses. Each No. 2 Pencil has a unique wooden pattern completely concealed by our signature yellow paint inspired by your kid’s ride to school. This model is widely available in the US.

Brunswick Green

Ready-To-Write™
